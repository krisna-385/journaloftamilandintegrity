The journal publishes research on Tamil language, classical Cankam (சங்கம்) literature, and translation studies, set side by side with scholarship on research integrity and publication ethics across the humanities.

Every accepted article carries a public review summary and a signed integrity statement from its authors — a record kept open, the way a palm-leaf manuscript keeps its string of pages in order.
